Pollution

Pollution has affected nature very badly in many ways. Air pollution is responsible for melting of glaciers, which is disturbing the balance of nature. The excessive use of plastic products and polythene bags are one of the main reason for increasing pollution. The increase in air pollution increases temperature across the globe. There are mainly four types of pollution- air pollution, water pollution, noise pollution and land pollution. Pollution spreads various types of diseases around the world. We must stop pollution.
