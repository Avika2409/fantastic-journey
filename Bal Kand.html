"Bal Kand" or "Bala Kanda" translates to the "Book of Childhood" or "Book of Youth" in English and is the first book of the Valmiki Ramayana and the Ramcharit Manas, an epic poem by Valmiki. The section narrates the early life and adventures of Lord Rama, including his birth, his journey with the sage Vishwamitra, and his marriage to Sita.
