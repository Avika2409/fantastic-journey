Kishkindha Kanda, also known as Kishkindha Kaandam, is the fourth book of the Ramayana, an ancient Sanskrit epic. It translates to the "Kingdom of Kishkindha" and primarily focuses on the alliance between Lord Rama and Sugriva, the exiled monkey king, and their search for Sita, Rama's wife, who was abducted by the demon King Ravana.
